What is Health Keeper?

Health Keeper is a unique service that helps you to stay healthy. It monitors your life parameters, like blood pressure, temperature, heart rate or skin resistance and immediately alerts if there a minor risk for any type of an accident.

With Health Keeper you can stare aware of any threats happening at this period of time around you. Travelling to another country? Check Health Pocket for a target location to find out, which medicine to grab with you.

Rumors about another flu epidemy? Check our service to find out, how many people are currently ill with this disease and decide, whether to take more vitamins.
